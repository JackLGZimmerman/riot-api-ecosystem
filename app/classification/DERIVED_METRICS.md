# Derived Metric Research Notes

These metrics are candidates for broad classification embedding experiments.
The active production subset is generated by the classification code path:
`physical_damage_share`, `magic_damage_share`, `damage_mitigated_ratio`,
`champion_damage_per_gold`, `damage_taken_per_gold`, `neutral_farm_share`, and
`champion_damage_focus`. The remaining definitions are research notes for
future focused probes.

Formula notation:

- `a + b` means the sum of smoothed source metrics.
- `a - b` means a linear difference of smoothed source metrics.
- `a / b` means a safe ratio of the two smoothed expressions.
